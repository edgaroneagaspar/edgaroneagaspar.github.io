I am a linguist working at the intersection of formal semantics, pragmatics, discourse theory and grammar. A central question in my work is how linguistic expressions shape the space of possible answers, alternatives and continuations in discourse.

My research has dealt with questions, exhaustivity, focus, topics, appositives, discourse particles, anaphora, conditionals, differential object marking and narrative suspense. Methodologically, the work combines formal analysis with experimental, corpus-based and comparative perspectives where appropriate.
